The Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System. It is a nearly perfect sphere of hot plasma, with internal convective motion that generates a magnetic field via a dynamo process.

The Orrery

An orrery is a mechanical model of the solar system that illustrates the positions and motions of the planets and moons. This 3D version allows you to visualize the solar system in motion.
